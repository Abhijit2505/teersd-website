Registration

8th International Conference on Advanced Computing, Networking, and Informatics (ICACNI 2025)

Author registration

Every accepted article should be registered by at least one author to appear in the proceedings. The acceptance list of articles will be announced in website and the authors will be intimated regarding the acceptance through e-mail. The details about events and dates of registration are given below:

Important Events regarding registration Date
Registration and Camera ready submission of the accepted articles 01-10 AUG 2025
Author registration with late fees of INR 1600 (Indian authors) or $30 (foreign authors) 11-13 AUG 2025

Registration includes ALL the following events to be completed by author within aforementioned deadline:

a. Preparing and uploading of the Camera Ready Article: The final camera-ready copy of the papers must be in Springer LNCS format with a length of maximum 10 pages. Over-length charges will apply for each extra page beyond 6 pages. However, it is desired that maximum number of pages in an article should not exceed 10 pages in any circumstance. Check Author Guidelines for formatting the camera ready article. Upload camera-ready PDF on to the Easychair, and submit source file(s) along with items mentioned in section e as .zip or .rar file.

b. Paying Registration Fee: Calculate the registration fee according to your page length and type of authorship as directed in REGISTRATION FEE DETAILS section and make payment using Foreign Telegraphic Transfer or Direct Transfer or NEFT Transfer or Wire transfer (preferable) or demand draft. Do not forget to keep the pay-in-slip or Challan or online payment receipt. You need the scanned copy of it or scan of the DD to complete next steps.

c. Filling up of the Registration Form, which can be found in MS WORD format [here] or in PDF format [here]

d. Filling up of the Consent to Publish Form [here]

e. Mailing the following items in .zip / .rar format to icacni2025@gmail.com and sambitbaksi@gmail.com:
1. The Camera ready Article source files (either MS word file, or all LaTeX files)
2. Scanned copy of filled in Registration Form
3. Scanned copy of filled in Consent to Publish Forms for both volumes
4. Proof of studentship (valid on date of submission of the manuscript) for availing Student Registration (if applicable) signed by head of the department/institute.
5. Scan of registration fee pay-in-slip/voucher of online transfer/DD (whichever applicable).

The mail must bear the subject line as : "Registration of article ICACNI2025-XXX". (A SINGLE MAIL must bear all items below. If size exceeds 20 MB, break the mail into more than one. Append to subject line of the mails: Part 1, Part 2 etc. Multiple mails without size limitation will not be considered.)

f. Submit the original DD (write your paper ID in the back side of the DD) with filled in registration form in person or by post so as to reach the following address
Dr. Alok Kumar Singh Kushwaha
Department of Computer Science & Engineering
School of Studies of Engineering & Technology
Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya, Bilaspur
Chhattisgarh, 495009, India

by above mentioned registration deadlines. YOU NEED NOT POST ANYTHING IF YOU HAVE NOT MADE PAYMENT USING DEMAND DRAFT. ICACNI will not be responsible for any postal delay or misplacement or failure in receiving the documents by the deadlines.

Registration fee details for authors:

To ensure publication of a paper in the Proceedings, at least one author has to register by submitting a normal registration fee within deadline as indicated below. Normal registration fee covers publication charges and lunch. The over-length charges beyond 6 pages (in LNCS format) will be added to the Normal registration fees to calculate the actual registration fee to be paid by the authors. The over-length charge is Rs. 800 (for Indian authors) or $15 (for foreign authors) per extra page for a maximum of four extra pages.

Category Indian Foreigner
Author - Student# INR 6000 US$ 200
Author - Academician INR 9000 US$ 230
Author - Industry-affiliate INR 11000 US$ 250

*Extra registration fee of INR 800 (for Indian authors) / US$ 15 (for foreign authors) is applicable per page beyond six pages.

Note:
# To avail student registration, the first or second author or the article MUST be a student enrolled in a full-time course under an institution with recognition. The author must submit the scanned copy of proof of studentship (on or after the date of submission of the manuscirpt).

Late author-registration will be considered until 31 AUG 2025 12:00 hrs IST with a charge of extra INR 1600 (Indian authors) or $30 (foreign authors) registration fee. No request of registration will be considered after this date.

B. Submit the original DD with filled in registration form in person or by post so as to reach Dr. Alok Kumar Singh Kushwaha, Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya, Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, 495009, India, by above mentioned registration deadlines. ICACNI will not be responsible for any postal delay or failure in receiving the documents by the deadlines.

Cancellation of Registration


If any author / listener wants to cancel his or her registration, he must write a mail to icacni2025@gmail.com and sambitbaksi@gmail.com on or before 01-AUG-2025 with a subject "Cancellation of Registration - ICACNI ". A processing fee of INR 2500 will be deducted from the paid registration fee and rest amount will be returned to the author / listener on 3` Jan 2020. No request of authorship cancellation and refund can be made after 01-AUG-2025.
