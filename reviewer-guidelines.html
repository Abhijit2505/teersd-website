Reviewer Guidelines

8th International Conference on Advanced Computing, Networking, and Informatics (ICACNI 2024)

Reviewer Guidelines

1. Login to EasyChair [You will be by default logging in as author and you will find this page]

2. Change role to 'Subreviewer' [Click 'ICACNI 2024' -> 'Change Role' -> Subreviewer]

3. Select article to review [You can see all articles assigned to you under 'Review requests']

4. Click 'Answer request'

5. Click 'I agree to review this submission' to review it. Deselect 'Send message' and click 'Select and/or Send Message'

6. Click 'Download submission' to download and read the article

7. Click 'Submit review' to review the article online. DO NOT click 'DOWNLOAD REVIEW FORM' and submit offline. WE REQUIRE ONLINE REVIEW.

8. Select 'Overall Evaluation' and 'Reviewer's Confidence', Fill 'Review' as your comments to authors. Fill 'Confidential remarks for the program committee' (if any), and Click 'Submit review' to complete the review process.
