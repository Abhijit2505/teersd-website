International Conference on

TRENDS IN ENERGY AND ENVIRONMENTAL RESEARCH FOR SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT [TEERSD-II 2024]

12th - 13th November 2024

[Organized by the Department of Chemical Engineering, Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya, Bilaspur]

The TEERSD is an international conference that aims to discuss the current trends and research in the field of energy and environmental management and how they are evolving to overcome challenges and prepare for future growth. The conference sessions will have keynote addresses by eminent personalities from industry and academia and oral presentations based on the session theme. This conference will provide an excellent opportunity for academicians, industrialists and the scientific community to exchange their ideas for cumulative growth and mutual benefit.
